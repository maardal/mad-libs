FAQ

What is madlibs?

Mad libs is a word game, often played in parties. You start with a pre-written story where words like verbs, nouns, adverbs and other descriptive words are blanked out. Your goal is to give/write quirky, funny or random words in place of these blank spaces. The story is then read aloud to roarous laughter by your peers. This game was created by Leonard Stern and Roger Price, all the way back in the 1950s!

What do you do to make a story?

Above, you see several sections, like "Who is the story about" or "And now some hilarous verbs". Your job is to write your funny words in text fields. There are suggestions in these text fields. When you have written an answer in every text field, then you can press the green button named "Make a story", and you will be transported to the next page, where your story is presented.
